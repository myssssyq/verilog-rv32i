RV32I Datapath Visualizer
Faithful to the attached minimal single-cycle core: 18 instructions, no pipeline, manual wire routing
stopped
Architecture summary: this app mirrors the attached Verilog core: pc → imem → decoder/immgen/regfile → aMux/bMux → alu → ram/wbMux → regfile, with explicit pcPlus4, branchTarget, jalrTarget, and pcNext. Control decisions come from the real decoder signals regWrite, memWrite, aluSrcA, aluSrcB, wbSel, branch, jump, and aluOp.
Layout strategy: fetch path stays on the left, decode and register data remain in the center, ALU and data memory stay on the right, and long feedback lines are routed around the top/bottom perimeter rather than through the middle.
